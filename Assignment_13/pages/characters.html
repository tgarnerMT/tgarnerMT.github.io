Characters

Characters in game are the units with which both the player and NPC's interact with each other.
These characters can be of varying races, but generally are divided into 4 categories:

  • Humanoid
  • Animal
  • Mech
  • Insect

Each type of character has its own behavioral quirks, defensive and offensive stats, and body parts that make up its bonus' and weaknesses.
