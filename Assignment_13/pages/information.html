Misc Information


Game Premise

Rimworld is a colony survival based game, meaning you 'control' multiple characters with the goal of surviving as a colony.
Rimworld's story involves multiple different origin tracks, but ultimately you are stranded for some reason and end up with 'x' people.
You'll have to avoid dying from events, starvation, disease, etc. all the while making sure that you grow so that you can build a spaceship to leave the planet.

Mechanics

Rimworld has several mechanics, but I'll list the most broad and commonly interacted with ones below:

  • Hunger
  • Sleep
  • Combat
  • Research
  • Mood (Happiness)

Playtime/Length

While the game has nearly infinite replayability, a single playthrough of a completely randomized campain could take over a hundred hours.
However, considering that the game has a near infinite amount of scenarios that can be played out, you can expect easily over a thousand hours.
